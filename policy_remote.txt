# Remote Work & Flexible Arrangements Policy

## Section 1: Policy Statement
Innovate Solutions Inc. supports flexible work arrangements, including remote work, to promote work-life balance.

## Section 2: Eligibility
Employees must have completed at least six (6) months of continuous service and maintain a "Meets Expectations" or higher rating in their most recent performance review to be eligible for a remote arrangement. Roles that require significant on-site presence are not eligible.

## Section 3: How to Request Remote Work
To request a remote work arrangement, an employee must submit a formal "Flexible Work Arrangement Proposal" to their direct manager and the HR department. This proposal must be submitted at least 30 days before the desired start date.

## Section 4: Proposal Contents
The proposal must outline:
- The specific arrangement requested (e.g., fully remote, 3 days remote / 2 days in-office).
- A justification for how productivity and communication will be maintained.
- A description of the proposed remote workspace, confirming it is safe and has reliable internet.
