# Company HR Policy

## Section 1: Overview
This document outlines the core Human Resources (HR) policies for all full-time employees at Innovate Solutions Inc.

## Section 2: Annual Leave (Paid Time Off - PTO)
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, accrued monthly. Leave must be requested at least two (2) weeks in advance via the company HR portal and requires manager approval. Unused leave up to a maximum of 5 days can be rolled over to the next calendar year.

## Section 3: Sick Leave
Employees are allotted 10 days of paid sick leave per year. For absences exceeding three (3) consecutive days, a medical certificate must be provided to HR. Sick leave does not roll over.

## Section 4: Parental Leave
We offer 16 weeks of paid primary caregiver leave and 4 weeks of paid secondary caregiver leave following the birth or adoption of a child.
